The MIA (Myco Investigation Agency) project, born to increase awareness that “the essential is invisible to the eyes”, has as its target the use of biological resources, such as fungi, in the fight against one of the most important environmental challenges such as pollution from chemical fertilizers. Although in scientific literature some fungal species (e.g. Pochonia chlamydosporia) are reported as being able to act as biofertilizers, the fungal universe stays largely unexplored….
